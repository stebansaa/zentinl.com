Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI video dubbing service

How natural do the AI-dubbed voices sound?

Our advanced AI technology preserves the voice characteristics, tone, emotion, and speaking style of the original speaker. The result is remarkably natural-sounding speech that maintains the authenticity of the original content while speaking a different language.

What languages do you support?

We currently support over 40 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese (Mandarin and Cantonese), Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, Hindi, and many more. If you need a specific language, please contact us to check availability.

How long does the dubbing process take?

The processing time depends on your video length and the number of target languages. Typically, a 10-minute video can be dubbed in a single language within a few hours. For enterprise clients with large volumes, we can establish dedicated processing queues for faster turnaround times.

Do you offer end-to-end service including publishing?

Yes, we provide a complete service from video sourcing to publishing. We can download your videos from YouTube or other platforms, dub them into your selected languages, and then handle the republishing to all major social platforms including metadata translation and optimization.

How does pricing work?

Our pricing is based on total video duration and the number of target languages. We offer volume discounts for enterprise clients and recurring projects. Please contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

Inspired by the Just-In-Time (JIT) production philosophy from Toyota, our Just on Time Video service generates videos exactly when they are needed. This approach minimizes waste, maximizes efficiency, and ensures that your video content is always timely and relevant.

Our service can be seamlessly applied across various domains, including Social Reels, Education, Marketing, and Sales, providing customized solutions that cater to specific needs and contexts.
