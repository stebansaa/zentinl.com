What Are Virtual Influencers?

Virtual influencers are AI-powered digital personalities that can create content, engage with audiences, and promote brands across social media platforms - without requiring any human intervention.

Unlike traditional influencers, virtual influencers never sleep, don't require physical photoshoots, and can be fully automated to create and publish content based on your brand guidelines.
